HOF HUSTLE 58 vs LORDS 44 (OBL) | Full Game Replay | February 24, 2024

The Lords U13 Gold Rep lose their first game of their four-game OBL weekend to the HOF Hustle 58-44. A good start overall by the Lords team who managed to go on a 9-2 run in the second quarter and contributed to a one point lead going into half-time. The third quarter was the turning point in the game where the Lords team just could not keep up and were down 10 after 3 quarters. The fourth was largely a back and fourth between both teams with HOF Hustle securing the game with a 14-point advantage.

Height was definitely an issue for the Lords team which showed up in rebounding stats 57-27 for HOF Hustle. A good defensive effort otherwise with plenty of steals by the Lords Gold team. Brandon was top points scorer for Lords with 12 points, and Adam K. contributed 10 points as well to the scoring effort.

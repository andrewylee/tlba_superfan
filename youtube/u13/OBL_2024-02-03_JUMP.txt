LORDS 69 vs JUMP BASKETBALL 64 (OBL) | Full Game Replay | February 3, 2024

The Lords U13 Gold Rep defeat Jump Basketball in overtime 69-64. A great back and forth match with plenty of lead changes, this game came right down to the wire! Tied initially 15-15 after the first quarter, the Lords team led by two by half-time, however Jump took back the lead by the end of the third by two points 45-43.

Both teams shot decently from the field at around 42% and 44% for Lords respectively. Having the tallest players on the floor certainly helped Jump's rebounding from an offensive rebounds perspective at 21 vs 13 and generally overall securing rebounds 40-34 over the Lords team.

Xavier led team scoring this game with 21 points along with 4 steals. Adam K. chipped in 15 on 6-12 (50%) shooting in the game, and Brandon also contributed 14 points.

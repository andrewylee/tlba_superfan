LORDS 66 vs EAST YORK EAGLES 70 (COALITION) | Full Game Replay | January 20, 2024

The East York Eagles defeat the Toronto Lords U13 Gold team 70-66 in a thrilling come from behind to force overtime. The Lords team continued to play with an even shorter bench than the morning. The Lords U13 team took a strong 13 point lead into the final quarter before strong play from the Eagles started turning the momentum around. Overall, the struggle around shooting and finishing around the rim continued in this game as it did in the morning.

NIAGARA RED REBELS 48 vs LORDS 62 (OBL) | Full Game Replay | February 24, 2024

The Lords U13 Gold Rep won their second game of the four-game OBL weekend against the Niagara Red Rebels 48-62. A strong effort by the entire team on both ends of the court throughout the entire match. The Lords team managed to take advantage of the 19 steals they were able to force the Niagara Red Rebels team to turnover! The game had a lot of fouls called to both teams as they were both very aggressive and active in the key.

Adam K. led the way in terms of scoring with 20 points, followed by Brandon with 14 points. The entire team contributed to the points boxscore on this game. The Lords team also rebounded very well and were led by Xavier (6 rebounds), along with Zach and Adrian who both had 5 boards each.

LORDS 53 vs IEM ALPHA ELITE 38 (IEM TOURNAMENT FINALS) | Full Game Replay | January 28, 2024

The Lords U13 Gold Rep team defeat the Alpha Elite in the finals of the "Pool I" IEM Tournament. The Lords Rep team proceeded to the afternoon finals after beating the Markham MUMBA in the final Sunday morning seeding round to face the also undefeated Alpha Elite team. The final was a highly anticipated game with the two undefeated Pool I teams battling it out for the championship!

Alpha Elite took the early lead while the Lords team kept things close and closed the quarter trailer by three - 9-12. The Lords team turned the tide in the second quarter, winningthat stretch 19-6 to take a commanding 28-18 lead into the half and never looking back. By the end of the game, the Alpha Elite team sustained a couple of injuries to key members plus fouled out a couple players and limped tot eh end with just 4 players on the court.

Overall the two teams shot 31.7% and 35.6% in favour of the Lords team. Adam K played the entire third quarter and was shooting very hot to earn his first MVP trophy during this stretch. Adam K. led the Lords U13 Gold team with 17 points along with 8 rebounds. Brandon also contributed heavily with 15 points along with Alex who chipped in 7 points along with a strong defensive effort! Congrats to the boys on this tournament championship!

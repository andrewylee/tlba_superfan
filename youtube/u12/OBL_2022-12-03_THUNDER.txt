ETOBICOKE THUNDER 44 vs LORDS 27 (OBL) | Full Game Replay | December 3, 2022

The Lords U12 team loses their first OBL game of the season, dropping to the Etobicoke Thunder 44-27 on Saturday December 3rd, 2022. The Thunder were aggressive from the start after losing to the Lords U12 team at their own Early Bird tournament! The Lords team could not get going during the 2nd quarter where they only managed to score one free throw during those 8 crucial minutes. Overall the Lords U12 team shot a season worst 11-for-52 (21.2%) in the game.

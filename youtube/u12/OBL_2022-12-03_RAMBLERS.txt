LONDON RAMBLERS 46 vs LORDS 43 (OBL) | Full Game Replay | December 3, 2022

The Lords U12 team lose their second straight OBL game of the season, dropping to their arch rivals London Ramblers 46-43 on Saturday December 3rd, 2022. The Lords team continued their shooting woes - just 25.8% from the field, and 50% from the line compared to the Ramblers who shot 40.4%. One bright side for the Lords U12 team was they out rebound the Ramblers 16-8.

Adam K. picked up most of the scoring slack and contributed 15 points in this loss. Special call-out to Alex who hauled in 9 rebounds off the glass - 4 offensively and 5 defensively!

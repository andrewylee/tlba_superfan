LORDS 56 vs WATERLOO WILDHAWK 53 (OBL) | Full Game Replay | February 19, 2023

The Lords U12 rebound on the second game of OBL Weekend #4 beating the Waterloo Wildhawks 56-53 on Sunday February 19th, 2023. The U12 faced-off against their new OBL "Pool C" rivals and were quickly outplayed once again and trailed 23-8 after one quarter. Thankfully the team was able to turnaround their play by picking up on defense and eliminating turnovers for extra opportunities - closing the gap to 39-40 by the end of the 3rd Quarter before edging by the Wildhawk's late in the 4th Quarter.

A blistering game by Adam K. who dropped 26 points which included 12 free-throws made. Brandon contributed 9 points along with 8 rebounds. Adam M. also contributed to the come-from-behind by hauling down 10 rebounds.

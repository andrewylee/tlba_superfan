LORDS 66 vs EAST YORK EAGLES 47 (Coalition) | Full Game Replay | November 26, 2022

The Toronto Lords U12 Rep team defeated the East York Eagles soundly 66-47, on Sunday November 26th, 2022. A great official start to the 2022/23 Coalition League and payback to the Exhibition loss a month ago! A great collective team effort by both the 1st shift starters and 2nd shift bench mob (39 by 1st shift, 27 by 2nd shift).

Adam K. lit it up during the game and led overall scoring for the Lords with 17 points during the game. Sydney and Xavier contributed a solid 14 and 12 points as well in the effort!
